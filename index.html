An Image Gallery

    My name is Trevour L Chidoti, I am a computer scientist and amatuer photographer from Harare, in Zimbabwe. I put together this image gallery for my 12th cs50 assignment.

    Learn more about cs50, Harvard's free online courses in computer science, on cs50's homepage on Edx.

    I travel a lot in Harare and when I do, I like to snap pictures of my favourites places, buildings and, sometimes, even people! So, for this assignment, decided to showcase a few of my favourite pictures that I, and some of my friends, have taken in Harare. I hope you like it. For more pictures you can visit my Instagram, or check out the pictures that I submitted for The Smithsonian Magazine photocontest.

    The National Social Security Association Headquarters in Harare (right), has one of the most beautiful buildings in the city.

    It's hundreds of large pane windows reflect the blue sky during the day, which gives the building a bluish-green hue on which is mesmerizing when juxtaposed with its grey concrete walls and the rest of the sky. It realy one of my favourite building to look at in town. Its at Corner Central Avenue and Sam Nujoma way in the CBD.
